Your questions,
answered.
It’s only natural for you to have questions, and here at Rumbo we want you to trust us. Here are some of the frequently asked questions that we’ve done our best to answer.
Is Rumbo available on all devices?
No, Rumbo is currently available on Android devices and will be coming to Apple and IOS devices soon.
Is Rumbo only available in Pretoria?
Currently, Rumbo only covers Pretoria and Capetown in South Africa, but is available all over the wolrd in countries such as England and Mexico.
Does Rumbo work like Uber or Bolt?
Rumbo is very similar to Uber and Bolt in that you can sarch for destinations and add stops, but is unique in the way that it works with only public transport.
How does Rumbo know how safe my area is?
Rumbo gathers information about an area through your reviews and our own gathered research, making your reviews very important to us and those around you. Learn more about this under Safety Information in the Features section.
Can I pay using the Rumbo app?
Public transport does not always cater to electronic methods of payment so instead, we have included a fair calculator that determines the cost of your trip for you so you always know how much you need to pay for your trip.
